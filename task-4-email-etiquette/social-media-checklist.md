# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## Do’s

* Maintain a professional and respectful profile
* Think before posting anything online
* Verify information before sharing
* Use privacy settings to protect your account
* Be respectful in comments and interactions

## Don’ts

* Do not share personal or sensitive information
* Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
* Do not engage in online arguments or hate speech
* Avoid spreading fake news or unverified content
* Do not misuse someone else’s content without permission
